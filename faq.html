What are the plot loan tax benefits?

Plot loan tax benefits can be availed if the investor decides to construct a villa, bungalow, or a house on it. If the plot remains as it is, then the investor is exempt from availing any tax loan benefits. For those who construct any type of home on their plot can use the following tax benefits to their use.

  • Tax benefit under Section 80C
  • Plot loan tax benefit under Section 24
  • Additional benefits [Deduction of interest rates & reduced interest rates for pre-construction]

Why do we have property taxes?

Property tax is levied on all owned real estate. The rate of property tax is based on the property value which is determined by the local municipal authorities. Paying property tax is compulsory and enforceable by law as the revenue generated is used to maintain and provide civic amenities such as road repairs, sewer system, lighting, maintenance of common civic areas and more

What is the property tax rate in Nagpur, and how is it calculated?

The property tax is a percentage of the actual value of the property which is based on the ready reckoner that is used to calculate stamp duty by the revenue department.

Property tax = Tax rate * Capital Value
Capital Value = Base value * built up area * usage * building type * age factor * floor factor

NMC allows its users to calculate property tax online by entering locality, area, usage, type, total plinth area and age of the property.
